This is the first note in the new notes workflow.

## What tends to work

- Make the choice opportunity explicit before the meeting starts.
- Keep the decision horizon short enough to preserve attention.
- Track unresolved problems across iterations, not just decisions taken.

## What tends not to work

- Treating every meeting as if the right participants are always present.
- Assuming decision closure means problem closure.
- Collapsing complex cross-functional issues into one silo-owned queue.

This note will evolve as more observations and examples are added.
