To the Bedrock

A portfolio of thoughts as notes, with related interactive modules doing explanatory and exploratory stories, animations, and visualisations.

It covers ideas about complexity, emergence, and the friction between how organisations describe their work and how that work actually behaves. Blended with science and philosophy, it tries to reach The Bedrock understanding all the way down.

Disclaimer: less grand and less rigorous than it might sound. Enjoy, and thanks for visiting!
