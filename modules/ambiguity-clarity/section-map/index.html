Clarity Map

The path from ambiguity to clarity, at a glance. Eight stations show handover readiness for PM, Design, and Dev. As you move down, ambiguity is reduced and work becomes more targeted.

Handover happens through artifacts: PM starts in Narrative and Brief. Design converges in Brief and Plan. Dev becomes build-ready in Story and Ticket. While Problem and Direction stay open, every brief still needs firm ground: improvement briefs anchor on Where We Are; new-feature briefs anchor on People.
