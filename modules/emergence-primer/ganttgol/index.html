GANTT meets Game of Life

This simulation puts a traditional delivery plan in direct contact with an emergent system. The Gantt side assumes linear progression, clean handoffs, and stable causality. The GoL side evolves through local interactions and feedback loops.

What This Is Saying

In hard predictive project models, weak signals are often treated as noise until late, and plans are managed as if the system is mostly closed. Modern product work is usually the opposite: open, coupled, and continuously perturbed by users, markets, operations, and adjacent teams.

Here, erosion is the point. Deterministic structure does not fail because planning is useless; it fails because interaction effects accumulate faster than static assumptions can hold. First contact with reality creates feedback, feedback amplifies, and the original phase logic starts to dissolve.
