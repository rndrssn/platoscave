What's a Garbage Can?

In this module, a garbage can is a choice opportunity (CO), often simply a meeting. In the full model there are four streams; here the CO is fixed so you can watch the other three streams move.

This animation is a simple concept demo. It illustrates the idea, not the full simulation logic used in Explore and Assess.
