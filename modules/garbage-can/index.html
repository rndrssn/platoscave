03 — The Garbage Can Model

Organised Anarchy

In 1972, Cohen, March and Olsen described organisations not as rational decision machines, but as garbage cans — collections of problems, solutions, participants and choice opportunities that collide more or less at random. Answer twelve questions about how decisions actually work where you are. The model will show you what kind of organised anarchy you are operating in.
