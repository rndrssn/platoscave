Concept Map

Software-development flow sits at the intersection of three traditions: queueing theory for the mathematics of waiting, complexity science for why local optimization breaks in non-linear systems, and modern agile management for the working practices that fall out of those constraints. The map shows how the concepts connect, the observations they predict, and the counter-intuitive results that fall out.
