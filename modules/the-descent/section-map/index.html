The descent, at a glance. Eight stations down the spine; three thresholds where the brief unlocks further kinds of work. Ambiguity lives at the top and is drained as you go down.

Every descent needs firm ground while Problem and Direction stay open. That ground — the anchor — lives in different places depending on whether the thing already exists.
