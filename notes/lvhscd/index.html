Low-volume-High-software content development

You cannot deploy a low-volume high-software content enterprising using an operational model designed deliver a high-volume high-software content enterprising.

The trick to low volume products is to optimize for low development cost, and not for the lowest possible BoM or build/manufacturing cost. One approach is to use off the shelf components as much as possible for both hardware and software instead of developing everything fully custom.
